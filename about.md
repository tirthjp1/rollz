# About Rollz Vancouver

We're a Vancouver franchise of [Rollz Ice Cream Canada]({{ site.parent_brand_url }}), bringing freshly rolled Thai-style ice cream to Main Street.

## What We Do

Our ice cream is made fresh on the spot — poured onto a frozen plate, mixed with your chosen ingredients, and rolled into beautiful curls right before your eyes. It's part dessert, part experience.

We offer **50+ rolled ice cream flavors**, plus waffles, milkshakes, and South Asian-inspired faloodas — all made with premium ingredients.

## Our Location

We're at **2685 Main St** in the heart of Vancouver's vibrant Main Street neighborhood. Whether you're grabbing a treat after dinner or looking for a fun outing, we've got you covered.

## Community

We're proud to be part of the Vancouver food scene. We source local BC ingredients whenever possible and love supporting our neighborhood.
